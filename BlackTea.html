Simple Delicious Tea Recipes

Black Tea

Description

This straightforward recipe focuses on brewing a perfect cup of black tea to enjoy on its own or with a splash of milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 tea bag or 1 teaspoon of loose black tea leaves
  • 1 cup of fresh, filtered water
  • Optional: sugar, honey, or a splash of milk to taste

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, bring the water to a boil.
  2. Add the tea leaves and sugar, and simmer for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in the milk and bring the mixture to a boil again.
  4. Once it boils, reduce the heat and simmer for 2-3 more minutes, or until it reaches a rich brown color.
  5. Strain the tea into a cup and serve hot.
